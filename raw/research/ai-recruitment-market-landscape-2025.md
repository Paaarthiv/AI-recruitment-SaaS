# AI Recruitment SaaS Market Landscape 2025

This source captures the initial market-landscape synthesis used to seed the wiki. It maps the AI recruitment market across talent intelligence, high-volume automation, structured assessment, and ATS/all-in-one platforms.

## Key Claims

- The AI recruitment market is growing, with AI features moving from premium differentiators toward baseline expectations.
- Enterprise talent intelligence is led by platforms such as Eightfold AI and Phenom.
- High-volume recruiting automation is led by conversational and agentic systems such as Paradox.
- SMB and early mid-market buyers remain underserved by deep, explainable AI recruiting tools.
- Regulatory pressure around automated employment decision tools is increasing the value of transparent, auditable AI.
