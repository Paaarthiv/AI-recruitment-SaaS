# Greenhouse

## Overview

Greenhouse is a well-established **applicant tracking system** (ATS) known for structured hiring methodology. Increasingly augmented with AI features, but primarily an ATS platform rather than an AI-native solution.

## Key Facts

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Segment** | Established ATS (AI-augmented) |
| **Target Market** | Mid-market to Enterprise |
| **Primary Focus** | Structured hiring workflows, broad integration ecosystem |

## Relevance

Major incumbent. Not AI-native, but a platform our product would need to integrate with or compete against. Many companies use Greenhouse as their ATS backbone and layer specialized AI tools on top.
