# Paradox

## Overview

Paradox is a **conversational AI platform** for high-volume hiring, best known for its AI assistant **"Olivia"**. It automates scheduling, screening, and candidate engagement through natural language conversations across SMS, web chat, and WhatsApp. The market leader in high-volume automation.

## Key Facts

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Segment** | High-Volume Automation / Conversational AI |
| **Target Market** | Enterprise & mid-market (high-volume roles) |
| **Primary Focus** | Administrative automation, scheduling, screening at scale |
| **Key Technology** | Conversational AI (NLP), workflow automation |
| **Key Product** | "Olivia" — AI assistant |
| **Competitive Position** | Leader in high-volume hiring automation |

## Relevance

Paradox is a **competitor in the automation space**. Key considerations:
- Dominant in high-volume/hourly hiring (retail, hospitality, logistics)
- Strong conversational UX — candidates interact via text message
- Less focused on deep talent intelligence or assessment quality
- Proves that AI assistants can handle end-to-end hiring workflows at scale
