# HireVue

## Overview

HireVue is a **video interview and assessment platform** focused on structured, compliant hiring. It combines video interviewing with game-based assessments and AI-driven evaluation, emphasizing auditability and bias mitigation. A top choice for regulated industries and enterprises with strict compliance requirements.

## Relevance

HireVue is a **competitor in the assessment space**. Key considerations:
- Strong compliance positioning — audit-ready, explainable decisions
- Faced controversy over facial analysis in interviews (pivoted away from this)
- Game-based assessments are innovative but niche
- Proves that compliance-first positioning can be a viable strategy
