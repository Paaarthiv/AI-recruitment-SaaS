# Eightfold AI

## Overview

Eightfold AI is an enterprise-focused **talent intelligence platform** that uses deep learning to match candidates to roles based on skills, potential, and career trajectory. It is one of the leading AI-native players in recruitment, positioned squarely in the enterprise segment.

## Key Facts

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Segment** | Talent Intelligence |
| **Target Market** | Enterprise |
| **Primary Focus** | Workforce planning, predictive skills matching, internal mobility |
| **Key Technology** | Deep learning, skills ontology, career pathing algorithms |
| **Competitive Position** | Market leader in enterprise talent intelligence |

## Relevance

Eightfold is a **direct competitor** in the talent intelligence space. Key considerations:
- Their deep learning approach to skills matching sets the bar for what enterprise buyers expect
- Internal mobility features (matching existing employees to open roles) is a sticky feature that increases retention
- Enterprise-only positioning leaves the SMB/mid-market underserved
