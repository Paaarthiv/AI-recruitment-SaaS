# Gem

## Overview

Gem is a **sourcing CRM** — a candidate relationship management tool that helps recruiters find, engage, and nurture talent pipelines. Positioned as a sourcing companion to existing ATS systems.

## Key Facts

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Segment** | Sourcing CRM |
| **Target Market** | Mid-market and enterprise recruiting teams |
| **Primary Focus** | Candidate outreach, nurturing, and pipeline CRM |
| **Competitive Position** | Adjacent workflow system rather than pure ATS |

## Relevance

Not a direct competitor, but in the adjacent sourcing CRM space. Our product may need to either integrate with or replace this kind of tool.
